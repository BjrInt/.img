.img | The simple image composer!

.img (pronounced “dot image”) is a powerful and versatile, yet easy to use image composer. Just add some pictures from your computer to your collection, and place them on your final composition. Crop, merge, add some text and effects and you're done! You can save your settings to create a series of preset pictures, useful for anyone looking to preserve a graphical identity.
